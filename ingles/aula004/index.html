Verbs

Verbo to be

O verbo to be pode significar "ser" ou "estar" e muda a flexão de acordo com o pronome.

verbo to be
Pronome Presente Passado Futuro
Singular I am was will
You are were
He, She, It is was
Plural We are were
You are were
They are were
